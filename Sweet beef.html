Sweet beef recipe

Home

Wanna try some Asian food? This is the sweet beef. It knows how to fullfill your sweetie and salty desire.

Those are the main ingredients:

Meat:

  • 1 pound of Beef (cut into bite-sized pieces).
  • 1 cup of Pineapple chunks (with juice).

Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup of Brown Sugar.
  • 1/4 cup of Vinegar (white or apple cider).
  • 1/4 cup of Soy Sauce.
  • 1 tablespoon of Ketchup.
  • 1 teaspoon of Garlic, minced.
  • 1 teaspoon of Ginger, minced.

Vegetables:

  • 1 Green Bell Pepper (cut into bite-sized pieces).
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper (cut into bite-sized pieces).
  • 1 Small Onion (cut into bite-sized pieces).
  • 2 tablespoons of Cornstarch (optional, for thickening).

Add some chili flakes to hotten it up!
