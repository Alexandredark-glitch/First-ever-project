Lasagna recipe

Home

This is the cheesiest lasagna in the world. And today, we are going to cook this beast.

Those are the main ingredients:

Meat:

  • 1 pound of Sweet Italian Sausage (bulk or links removed from casing).
  • 3/4 pound of Lean Ground Beef.

Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup of chopped Onion.
  • 2 cloves of Garlic, crushed.
  • 1 (28 ounce) can of Crushed Tomatoes.
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans of Tomato Paste.
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans of Tomato Sauce.
  • 1/2 cup of water.
  • 2 tablespoons of Sugar.
  • 2 teaspoons of Dried Basil Leaves.
  • 1/2 teaspoon of Fennel Seeds.
  • 1 teaspoon of Italian Seasoning.
  • 1 tablespoon of Salt.
  • 1/4 teaspoon of Ground Black Pepper.
  • 4 tablespoons of Chopped Fresh Parsley.

Cheese:

  • 16 ounces of Ricotta Cheese.
  • 1 Egg.
  • 3/4 teaspoon of Salt.
  • 3/4 pound of Mozzarella Cheese, sliced.
  • 3/4 cup of Grated Parmesan Cheese.

    • Do not hesitate to add the cheese honey!
